02 Automation

Autonomous Job Aggregator

Challenge

Junior developers waste hours manually scrolling through job boards dominated by senior-level posts requiring 5+ years of experience they don't have.

Solution

AI agent scrapes LinkedIn, Naukri, and Indeed via RSS gateways. Groq filters junior-appropriate roles, deduplicates results, and auto-inserts into PostgreSQL daily.

Tech Stack
Python Groq psycopg2 RSS AOL Gateway

Results: 53+ curated junior developer jobs updated every day. Deduplication system prevents repeat entries. Runs fully autonomously without manual intervention.
